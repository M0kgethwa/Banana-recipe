Banana Bread Recipe

Banana bread is a moist, sweet, cake-like bread made from mashed overripe bananas. It's perfect for using up bananas that are too brown to eat and works as breakfast, a snack, or dessert.

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 55 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • Optional: Chopped nuts, chocolate chips, or cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the bananas.
  3. Mix in melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  4. Stir in baking soda and salt.
  5. Mix in the flour until just combined.
  6. Pour into loaf pan and bake for 50–60 minutes.
  7. Let it cool, then slice and enjoy.
